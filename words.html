With the internet at our fingertips most of the time, there is no excuse for a poor vocabulary. The next time someone asks you how you are doing, don't answer with a bland "Fine." Go find what you really mean. Maybe your answer will be "Lovely." Or perhaps you will find you are really pulchritudinous.
