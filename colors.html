Family of Man

The Family of Man exhibit is permanently installed in Clervaux Castle in Luxembourg. The Steichen collections website gives you an idea of how astonishing this exhibit is.

steichencollections.lu
Photos: Gwen Davis 2019

The Family of Man is the most widely seen exhibition in the history of photography. First shown in 1955, the exhibition traveled throughout the United States and to 46 countries and was seen by more than nine million people.

This exhibit was first shown at the Museum of Modern Art in New York. Scroll down to the Installation Images section to get actual photos.
